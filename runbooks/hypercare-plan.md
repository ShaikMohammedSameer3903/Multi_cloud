# Hypercare Support Plan

This document outlines the post-rollout stability operations, daily sync-ups, and platform exit criteria.

---

## 🏥 1. 30-Day Hypercare Support Plan

* **Objective**: Ensure platform stability post-rollout and resolve transient alert issues.
* **Duration**: 30 days (Day 1 to Day 30 post go-live).
* **Support Model**:
  - **Tier 1**: Incident responders monitoring the dashboard.
  - **Tier 2**: Azure Architects on-call for policy adjustments.
  - **Daily Review Checkpoint**: 09:00 EST daily checkpoint to review cost anomalies and PIM log entries.
* **Exit Criteria**:
  - 100% database backup success over 7 consecutive days.
  - Zero critical alert false positives.
  - Budget variances within +/- 5% of monthly forecasts.
